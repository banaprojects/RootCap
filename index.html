Who Are We

The Horizon Lab is a dynamic team of five passionate innovators united by a shared mission to tackle one of the most pressing challenges of our time: food insecurity. Our focus is particularly sharp on Africa, a continent where millions face the daily struggle of accessing sufficient and nutritious food. Among the regions we prioritize is South Sudan, a country where the crisis of food insecurity has reached alarming levels, affecting countless lives and communities.

Our Purpose

Mission Statement

"The Horizon Lab's mission is to solve food insecurity in South Sudan through strategic agricultural improvements that promote sustainable practices, empower local communities, and foster innovation through collaborative partnerships."

Problem Statement

"South Sudan faces one of Africa's most severe food insecurity crises, with 57% of the population (7.7 million people) experiencing acute food insecurity projected by mid-2025. This crisis is directly connected to the country's underperforming agricultural sector, which suffers from limited agricultural productivity, vulnerability to climate shocks, disrupted food production due to conflict, economic instability, and insufficient infrastructure. In Pibor County, one of the hardest-hit regions, recurrent flooding and conflict have devastated croplands, with local farmers achieving yields 60-70% below potential and 80% of households regularly skipping meals. The nutrition crisis is equally alarming, with 2.1 million children and 1.1 million pregnant or breastfeeding women facing acute malnutrition. Without targeted interventions to strengthen agricultural systems and practices, this cycle of food insecurity will continue to endanger millions of lives and further destabilize the country."
